New York Governor

Andrew M. Cuomo’s

commitment to the recovery of Puerto Rico post Hurricane María efforts has been extraordinary.

Under the NY Stands with Puerto Rico initiative, the Governor has played an unprecedented role in championing Puerto Rico’s recovery in the aftermath of María.

The RISE 2019 Conference Leadership Committee is proud to recognize Governor Cuomo’s remarkable commitment to and support of the people of Puerto Rico this upcoming November.
